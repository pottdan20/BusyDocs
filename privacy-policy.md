# Privacy Policy

**Busy**
**Effective Date:** March 29, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 29, 2026

Busy ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Busy mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Google Account:** When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo.
- **Profile Information:** Your display name and avatar, which you may update at any time.
- **User Content:** Events you create, bucket list items, comments, friend groups, and related content you post through the App.

### Information We Access With Your Permission
- **Google Calendar (read-only):** If you grant access, we read your Google Calendar events to determine when you are busy. We do not modify, create, or delete any events on your Google Calendar.
- **Device Calendar:** If you enable device calendar sync, we read events from calendars you select on your device to determine your availability.
- **Push Notifications:** If you allow notifications, we store a device token to send you event invites, friend requests, and comment notifications.
- **Camera & Photo Library:** If you choose to upload a profile photo, group image, or event image, we request access to your camera or photo library. Photos are uploaded to our servers and stored securely.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Calendar Busy Blocks:** Start and end times of calendar events are cached on our servers to calculate availability. We store event titles only if you enable "show details" in your calendar preferences. No other event metadata (attendees, descriptions, locations, attachments) is stored.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Authenticate your account and maintain your session
- Display your profile to friends you connect with
- Show your calendar availability to friends you choose to share it with
- Find mutually free times among groups of friends
- Send push notifications for event invites, friend requests, and comments
- Display your events and calendar on the App's calendar view

We do **not**:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Use your data for advertising or ad targeting
- Share your calendar details with anyone you have not explicitly added as a friend
- Access your Google Calendar for any purpose other than determining free/busy times

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### With Other Users
- Your **display name and profile photo** are visible to other Busy users who search for you or are connected with you.
- Your **availability status** (free/busy, not event details) is visible only to friends or groups you explicitly select.
- **Event details** are visible only to the event creator and invited participants.
- **Comments** are visible to event or bucket list item participants.

### With Service Providers
We use the following third-party services to operate the App:
- **Supabase** (database, authentication, file storage) — [supabase.com/privacy](https://supabase.com/privacy)
- **Google** (OAuth sign-in, Calendar API) — [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Expo** (push notification delivery, app builds) — [expo.dev/privacy](https://expo.dev/privacy)
- **Apple** (TestFlight and App Store distribution) — [apple.com/privacy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/)

We do not share your personal information with any other third parties.

---

## 4. Data Security

- Google Calendar OAuth tokens are encrypted using AES-256 encryption before storage.
- All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS.
- Database access is protected by Row-Level Security policies — users can only access their own data and data explicitly shared with them.
- Session tokens are stored in your device's secure storage.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Calendar busy blocks** are cached for up to one year and replaced on each sync.
- **Account data** is retained for as long as your account is active.
- **Expired availability statuses** (e.g., "open to hang") are automatically deleted within 5 minutes of expiration.

---

## 6. Your Rights & Choices

### Access & Control
- You can view and update your profile information at any time in the App.
- You can revoke Google Calendar access by disconnecting in the App's settings or in your Google Account permissions at [myaccount.google.com/permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions).
- You can disable device calendar sync at any time in the App's settings.
- You can disable push notifications in your device's system settings.

### Account Deletion
You can delete your account at any time from the App's profile screen. Account deletion permanently removes:
- Your profile, display name, email, and avatar
- All events you created and related invites
- All friendships and group memberships
- All calendar data, comments, bucket list items, and availability records
- Your Google Calendar token and push notification token

Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.
